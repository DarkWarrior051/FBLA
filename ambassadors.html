How to Become a Student Ambassador

If you are passionate about mental health and want to make a positive impact, becoming a Student Ambassador is a great way to get involved. As an ambassador, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Promote mental health awareness in your school or community.
  • Organize and participate in SOS events and campaigns.
  • Lead initiatives that support mental health resources and self-care practices.

To apply, fill out the ambassador application form on our website or contact us at info@sosinitiative.org.
