Directions

The arena is located at Commack High School.

  • From NYC: Travel approximately 54.1 miles via I-495 E. Take Exit 52 for Commack Rd, follow signs to Commack High School.

Seating Chart

Our arena offers a variety of seating options:

  • General Admission: Open seating in designated areas.
  • VIP Seating: Premium seats with the best views and exclusive access.
  • Accessibility: Wheelchair-accessible seating and facilities available.

Policies

  • No outside food or beverages allowed.
  • All attendees must present a valid ticket for entry.
  • Smoking and vaping are prohibited inside the arena.
  • Bag checks are conducted at all entrances for security purposes.

Amenities

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the arena.
  • Concession stands with a variety of food and drink options.
  • Restrooms available on all levels.
  • First aid stations for medical assistance.
